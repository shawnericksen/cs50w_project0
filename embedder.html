Who We Are


Embedder is an early-stage startup focused on solving a pain-point felt by many students, young professionals, and self-learners: Networking. Building new skills is easier than ever with hundreds of services and platforms catering to accessible mass-education, but very few have addressed the need to connect directly with individuals who can provide active, substantive feedback. Through the use of innovative technologies and modern platforms, we seek to revolutionize the way people find and connect with specialists in the fields that they are working to break into, from Computer Science to Real Estate.
