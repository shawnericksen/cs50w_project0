Our Mission


One of the biggest facets of the response to COVID will be, following the development of a vaccine, its effective distribution throughout the United States, most especially in highly diverse, very dense urban settings. Thus, this project will center on equitable distribution of vaccines in and around the city of Chicago. In practice, this will look like two short-term deliverables -- an actionable policy brief and paired technical tools that can be utilized by local governments and community organizations. Longer-term, we are developing a technical assistance organization that will coordinate vaccine distribution and tool usage in light of policy recommendations.
